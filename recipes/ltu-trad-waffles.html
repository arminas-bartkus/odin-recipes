Traditonal Lithuanian Waffle Pancakes

    Back Home

Description

One of my absolute favourites, nothing screams Home like Mama's traditional pancakes. So simple to make and yet so, so, delicious. The recipe is so simple, you'll be watching the waffle iron longer than making the actual batter!

Ingredients (1 portion)

  • 200g flour
  • 200g sour cream
  • 100g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 10g vanilla sugar
  • Salt

Steps by step guide

  1. Whip up the eggs. Stir flour, sour cream, sugar and melted butter. Mix everything well.
  2. Heat the frying pan, and apply fat on it.
  3. Heat the frying pan with a bit of fat, add one tablespoon of the mixture and bake it until it is beautifully browned. Serve it with sour cream and the desired jam.
